Clean Street of Gibraltar
Clean Street of Gibraltar

Barangay Gibraltar's Clean and Green project started since the 1990s, and since then, it makes a great impact in maintaining the cleanliness in the barangay.

Clean

Street Cleaning
Street Cleaning
Street Cleaning

Barangay Gibraltar has undeniably clean surroundings, and that is because the barangay and its officials keep their surroundings clean through regular cleanings. They have employed street cleaners to facilitate the sanitation of the barangay. There are also regular cleanings in watersheds, parks, and streets that take place every last Saturday of the month which is participated by barangay officials and volunteers.

Green

Tree Planting
Tree Planting
Tree Planting
Tree Planting

Trees play an important part in preserving the environment. The barangay officials together with Environmental Management Bureau, and its constituents help in preserving the environment by regularly planting trees during rainy season. Seedlings are provided by Environmental Management Bureau. Tree planting is mostly done in vacant lots that are government property.
