Barangay Gibraltar and its officials are dedicated in preserving their barangay's environment. To show their dedication for this advocacy, they have organized barangay rules and activities that are vital to lessen the the harmful impact of the daily activities on the environment. Their two main environmental protection projects are Waste Segregation and the Clean and Green Project. Besides these two projects, the barangay also adapts the environmental ordinances present in Baguio city and laws that govern the entire Philippines.
