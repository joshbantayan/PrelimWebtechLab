Barangay Gibraltar is one of the largest Barangay in Baguio city since it has seven puroks. This barangay can be one of the busiest barangays in the city during the holidays due to its near location to the tourist spots. Even at its busiest season, Gibraltar still keep tracks of its own environment. The beauty of this barangay's environment is undeinably evident in their luscious trees and clean surroundings. Barangay Gibraltar and its officials wish to maintain this by issuing rules and projects in the entire barangay.
