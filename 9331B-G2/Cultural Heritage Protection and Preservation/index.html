THE CULTURE

The Cordilleran’s way to showcase their culture is dancing while playing instruments like “gangsa” and “solibao”. These dances are usually done when a special occasion takes place. Barangay Gibraltar is rich of culture about the Cordilleran Region. Most of the residence of the barangay are indigenous people from Cordillera like the Ibaloy, Kankanaey, Ifugao, Kalinga, Apayao, and Bontoc. The common term for the highlanders is “Igorot” but natives prefer to call them by their respective tribe names as mention before. Although there are some residence that are lowlanders that mostly came from Pangasinan. Activities concerning culture are attended by the residence whether they are a highlanders or lowlanders.
